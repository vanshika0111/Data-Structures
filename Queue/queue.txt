Queue (FIFO) has two pointers --> Rear and front

Generally, rear is used for deletion while front is used for insertion.

Operations applied in queue are:
	1. Enqueue ~ insertion
	2. Dequeue ~ deletion

Types of queues:
	1. Linear/simple queue
	2. Circular queue
	3. Deque/ Double-ended queue
	4. Priority queue (sscending & descending priority queue)
	5. Multiple queue

Applications of queue:
	1. Josephus problem
