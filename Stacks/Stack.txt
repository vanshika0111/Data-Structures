Stack (LIFO) has one pointer --> TOP

Stack can be implemented using arrays or linked lists.

Operations performed in stack are:
	1. push ~ insertion
	2. pop ~ deletion
	3. peek ~ display top-most element

Types of stacks:
	1. simple stack
	2. multiple stack

Applications of stack are:
	1. Reversing a list
	2. Parantheses checker
	3. Conversion: infix to postfix
	4. Evaluation of a postfix
	5. Conversion: infix to prefix
	6. Evaluation of a prefix
	7. Recursion
	8. Tower of Hanoi
